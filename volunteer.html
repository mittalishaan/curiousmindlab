Volunteer With Us

Help remove barriers to curiosity.

CuriousMindLab welcomes students and mentors who want to teach, create resources, support outreach, and make STEM learning more accessible.

🧑‍🏫 Tutor

Help students learn math, physics, or computer science through free sessions.

📚 Create Resources

Write notes, problems, explanations, or study guides for students.

💻 Build Technology

Help improve our website, tools, and accessibility-focused projects.

🌎 Outreach

Connect with schools, libraries, and organizations that serve students with fewer opportunities.

Who should volunteer?

We welcome people who are patient, kind, reliable, and excited to help students grow. You do not need to be perfect at every subject; you just need a willingness to serve and learn.
