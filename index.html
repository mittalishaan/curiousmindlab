Apply as a Student Become a Volunteer

Talent is universal. Opportunity is not.

CuriousMindLab exists to help students whose circumstances limit access to STEM opportunities by providing free mentorship, educational resources, and a supportive learning community.

How it works

1. Apply

Students complete a short application describing their interests and learning needs.

2. We review

We prioritize students facing financial, geographic, accessibility, or mentorship barriers.

3. Learn

Students join free sessions, receive resources, and build confidence in STEM.

4. Give back

As the community grows, learners can become mentors for future students.
