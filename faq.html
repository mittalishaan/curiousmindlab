Frequently Asked Questions

Is CuriousMindLab free?

Yes. Our goal is to provide free STEM learning opportunities for students facing barriers to access.

Who can apply?

Our programs are designed primarily for students whose circumstances limit access to STEM education, including financial, geographic, accessibility, or mentorship barriers.

What subjects do you offer?

We currently focus on mathematics, physics, and computer science.

Do I need prior experience?

No. Beginners are welcome. Curiosity matters more than background.

How can I volunteer?

You can help by tutoring, creating resources, building technology, or supporting outreach.
